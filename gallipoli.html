Gallipoli.

Gallipoli: The scale of our war (Until 2018) Developed by Te Papa working closely with Weta Workshop, Gallipoli: The scale of our war takes visitors on an immersive journey through the triumphs and tragedies of the Gallipoli campaign, as told through the eyes and words of the ordinary New Zealanders who were there.

This incredible exhibition will be on for the next four years, to coincide with the WWI centenary commemorations.
